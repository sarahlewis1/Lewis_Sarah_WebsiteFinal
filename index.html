About Us

We are chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores. As a married couple, we wanted to create a restaurant that reflects the perfect blend of both of us. By fusing Genie's pastry expertise with Timothy's authentic Filipino roots, we were able to fulfill our dreams and represent the culture we truly love.

Why Kasama?

In Tagalog, Kasama means “together,” and we chose to stand by that. When starting our restaurant, we wanted to meld two cultures together by bringing authentic Filipino flavors to the heart of Chicago. Now, we are fortunate enough to serve inventive Filipino cuisine and top-tier pastries for all to enjoy.
